## TIER 3: ULTRA AGGRESSIVE (Maximum Growth + Alpha Generation)

### 1. ASSET ALLOCATION
**Target: 85% Equities / 10% Alternatives / 5% Cash**

**Core Equities (50%):**
- **20%** - Invesco QQQ Trust (QQQ) - Technology leadership
- **15%** - SPDR S&P 500 ETF (SPY) - Market beta
- **10%** - ARK Innovation ETF (ARKK) - Disruptive innovation
- **5%** - VanEck Semiconductor ETF (SMH) - Chip cycle

**Tactical/Sector Rotation (35%):**
- **Rotates** among 11 S&P sectors based on momentum ranking
- **Maximum per sector:** 15% of equity allocation
- **Minimum holding period:** 2 weeks

**Alternatives (10%):**
- **5%** - ProShares Ultra S&P500 (SSO) - 2x leveraged S&P
- **3%** - MicroStrategy (MSTR) - Bitcoin proxy
- **2%** - Bitcoin ETF (IBIT) - Direct crypto exposure

**Cash (5%):** For day trading and opportunistic buys

### 2. ENTRY RULES
**Multi-Timeframe Confirmation Required:**
1. **Weekly Chart:** Uptrend (price > 20-week SMA > 50-week SMA)
2. **Daily Chart:** 
   - MACD bullish crossover + histogram turning positive
   - RSI > 50 and rising
   - Volume surge on breakout (>150% of 20-day average)
3. **60-minute Chart:** 
   - Pullback to 20-period EMA with bullish divergence
   - Stochastic %K crossing %D from oversold (<30)

**Sector-Specific Filters:**
- **Momentum Rank:** Top 3 sectors by 3-month performance
- **Relative Strength:** Sector ETF vs SPY > 1.05
- **Breakout Confirmation:** New 20-day high on above-average volume

### 3. EXIT RULES
**Aggressive Stop Losses:**
- **Individual Stocks:** 8% hard stop (no exceptions)
- **Sector ETFs:** 6% trailing stop (daily close)
- **Leveraged ETFs:** 5% maximum loss
- **Portfolio Level:** 8% drawdown → reduce leverage, increase cash to 15%

**Profit Taking:**
- **Core Positions:** 10% gain → raise stop to breakeven
- **Tactical Positions:** 15% gain → take 1/2 position off
- **Momentum Plays:** 25% gain → full exit, wait for reset
- **Maximum Run:** 40% gain → mandatory profit taking

### 4. REBALANCING  
- **Frequency:** Weekly review, monthly execution
- **Thresholds:** ±10% drift triggers immediate rebalance
- **Momentum Reset:** Any position down 15% from purchase → mandatory sell
- **Cash Recycling:** Profits >20% recycled into new momentum leaders

### 5. LEAPS STRATEGY
**Allocation:** 15-20% of portfolio in LEAPS

**Delta Targets:**
- **Growth Plays:** 0.65-0.75 delta (balanced risk/reward)
- **Momentum Plays:** 0.80-0.90 delta (high conviction)
- **Maximum Theta:** -0.03 daily time decay per contract

**Expiry Selection:**
- **Primary:** 24-month expirations (maximum time premium)
- **Secondary:** 12-18 months for tactical plays
- **Avoid:** <9 months to expiration

**Rolling Rules:**
- **Profit Roll:** 75%+ gain → roll to capture profits
- **Time Roll:** 9 months to expiration → evaluate roll/extend
- **Delta Management:** Roll up/down to maintain 0.70-0.80 delta
- **Volatility Adjust:** High IV (>40%) → sell premium, roll to lower IV strikes

### 6. OPTIONS STRATEGY
**Covered Calls (Income Generation):**
- **Underlying:** Core equity positions held >30 days
- **Strike Selection:** 10-15% OTM for 30-45 DTE
- **Delta Target:** 0.30-0.40 delta (30-40% probability ITM)
- **Premium Minimum:** 1% of underlying value per month
- **Rolling:** At 21 DTE or 50% profit

**Credit Spreads (Premium Collection):**
- **Iron Condors:** On range-bound indices (VIX < 20)
  - 16 delta short strikes, 30 DTE
  - 1:1 risk/reward, close at 50% max profit
- **Bull Put Spreads:** In uptrends
  - 30 delta short put, 15 delta long put
  - 30-45 DTE, close at 50% profit
- **Maximum Allocation:** 10% of portfolio to credit strategies

**Greeks Limits:**
- **Delta:** Net portfolio delta ≤ 0.85 (85% market exposure)
- **Theta:** Target +0.1% daily (positive time decay)
- **Vega:** Net vega ≤ 5% of portfolio (limited volatility risk)
- **Gamma:** Manage gamma risk in last 10 DTE

### 7. DAY TRADING RULES
**Setup Criteria (ALL must be present):**
1. **Pre-Market Gappers:** ±3% move on >2x average volume
2. **Technical Alignment:** 
   - 5-minute chart: Price > VWAP, RSI > 50
   - 1-minute chart: Bullish candle pattern
3. **Level 2 Support:** Bid/ask spread <0.10%, depth at key levels
4. **News Catalyst:** Earnings, FDA, partnership announcements

**Risk Management:**
- **Risk per Trade:** 1% of day trading capital ($1,000 on $100k)
- **Position Sizing:** Stop loss distance determines shares
  - Formula: Risk $ / (Entry - Stop) = Shares
- **Maximum Daily Loss:** 3% of day trading capital → STOP TRADING
- **Maximum Daily Trades:** 5 trades maximum
- **Win Rate Minimum:** 40% required to continue day trading

**Entry/Exit Rules:**
- **Entries:** Only on pullbacks to VWAP/EMA in direction of trend
- **Exits:** 
  - 2:1 risk/reward minimum
  - 50% at 1R, move stop to breakeven
  - 50% runner with trailing stop
- **Time Filters:** No new entries after 2:00 PM EST
- **Overnight Holds:** Never hold day trades overnight

### 8. SECTOR ROTATION
**Ranking Methodology (Weekly):**
1. **Price Momentum (40% weight):** 3-month total return
2. **Relative Strength (30% weight):** Sector vs SPY ratio
3. **Earnings Momentum (20% weight):** EPS estimate revisions
4. **Technical Health (10% weight):** % of stocks above 50-day MA

**Sector Scoring:**
- **Top Tier (Buy):** Score > 70 → allocate 10-15%
- **Middle Tier (Hold):** Score 50-70 → allocate 5-10%
- **Bottom Tier (Avoid):** Score < 50 → 0% allocation

**Rotation Triggers:**
- **Monthly Rebalance:** Adjust allocations based on latest scores
- **Emergency Rotation:** Any sector drops 2 tiers → immediate exit
- **Momentum Confirmation:** Sector must be in top 3 for 2 consecutive weeks
- **Maximum Churn:** 25% portfolio turnover per month limit

**Sector-Specific Rules:**
- **Technology (XLK):** Requires NASDAQ > 200-day SMA
- **Financials (XLF):** Requires yield curve > 0 (not inverted)
- **Energy (XLE):** Requires oil > $70 and rising
- **Healthcare (XLV):** Requires VIX > 18 (defensive play)

## RISK MANAGEMENT PROTOCOLS (ALL TIERS)

### 1. POSITION SIZING
- **Conservative:** Max 5% per position, 15% per sector
- **Moderate:** Max 4% per position, 12% per sector  
- **Ultra Aggressive:** Max 3% per position, 10% per sector

### 2. DRAWDOWN CONTROLS
- **Level 1 (-5%):** Review all positions, tighten stops
- **Level 2 (-8%):** Reduce equity exposure by 25%
- **Level 3 (-12%):** Move to 50% cash, reassess strategy
- **Level 4 (-15%):** Full risk reset - all positions closed

### 3. VOLATILITY ADJUSTMENTS
- **VIX > 25:** Reduce equity exposure by 20%
- **VIX > 30:** Reduce equity exposure by 40%, increase cash
- **VIX > 40:** Maximum defensive posture (70%+ cash/bonds)

### 4. MARKET REGIME FILTERS
- **Bull Market (SPY > 200-day SMA):** Full allocation
- **Correction (SPY 10-20% below highs):** 50-75% allocation
- **Bear Market (SPY >20% below highs):** 25-50% allocation
- **Crisis (SPY >30% below highs):** 10-25% allocation, focus on quality
