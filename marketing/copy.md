1. **What Are LEAPS?**
```
LEAPS = Long-term Equity AnticiPation Securities

They're call options that expire 1-3 years out.

Why we love them:
• 4x leverage without margin
• Defined downside (premium paid)
• Unlimited upside
• No liquidation risk

It's how we turn $10K into $40K exposure.
```

2. **Tax-Loss Harvesting Explained**
```
Tax-loss harvesting in plain English:

You sell a losing position to realize the loss.
Immediately buy something similar (avoid wash sale).
Use the loss to offset gains elsewhere.

Result: Same market exposure, lower tax bill.

Our OPTIMIZER agent does this automatically. 🧾
```

3. **Sector Rotation 101**
```
Sector Rotation = ride the winners, dump the losers

Markets move in cycles:
Tech leads → rotate to tech
Energy surges → rotate to energy
Healthcare lags → rotate OUT

Our STRATEGIST scans 11 sectors daily.
Always positioned in top 3 momentum leaders.
```

4. **Covered Calls for Income**
```
Covered Calls = rent out your stocks

You own 100 shares of AAPL.

Collect premium ($200-$500/month).

If stock doesn't move → keep premium
If stock rallies past strike → sell shares at profit + premium

It's how we generate income while waiting.
```
