Questions?

Why do you need access to my brokerage?
So our AI agents can view your portfolio, execute trades, and manage positions on your behalf. We use read + trade permissions (not withdrawal).

Can you withdraw my money?
No. We only have trade permissions. Your money stays in your account and can only be withdrawn by YOU.

What if I want to disconnect?
You can revoke access anytime in your Cortex dashboard or your broker's settings. Your positions remain yours — you can manage them manually.
