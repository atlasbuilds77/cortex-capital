### 6. Understanding Portfolio Beta
```
Beta = how volatile your portfolio is vs. the market

Beta 1.0 = moves with market
Beta 1.5 = 50% more volatile
Beta 0.5 = 50% less volatile

Our typical portfolios: Beta 1.1-1.3

Why?
Higher beta = higher potential returns
But also higher drawdowns

We use stop-losses and position sizing to manage the downside.

Risk-adjusted returns > raw returns.

#RiskManagement #PortfolioTheory
```
